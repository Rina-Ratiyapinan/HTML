The Foodaholic

"Uncontrollable craving for food" Meet The Foodaholic, a group of close-knit friends united by their shared love for exploring the world of flavors. With adventurous palates and a passion for discovering new cuisines, they have made it their mission to seek out the most unique and delicious dishes from around the globe. Whether it is street food stalls, trendy restaurants, or hidden gems off the beaten path, this crew of food enthusiasts is always ready to embark on their next culinary adventure. Their hobby? Tasting it all and sharing honest, fun reviews on their blog, where they capture the essence of each dish and offer recommendations to fellow food lovers. From spicy street tacos to decadent desserts, their blog is a treasure trove for anyone eager to explore the diverse world of food.

Exploring local food and restaurant

Every week, The Foodaholic takes their passion for food to the next level by visiting a new restaurant somewhere in Finland. Their goal? To sample the country's rich and diverse culinary scene, from cozy local bistros to high-end dining spots, and everything in between. Whether they are indulging in traditional Finnish dishes like reindeer stew and Karjalanpiirakka, or sampling international cuisines with a Nordic twist, the group is always on the lookout for exciting flavors. Each restaurant visit is carefully documented on their blog, where they share in-depth reviews, rating everything from taste and presentation to atmosphere and service. It is their way of exploring Finland through its food, one meal at a time.
